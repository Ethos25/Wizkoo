Legislative status — April 2026
California does not have an Education Savings Account program. There is no ESA legislation pending in the California legislature as of April 2026. California also does not offer vouchers, tuition tax credits, or other publicly funded school choice programs that function similarly to ESAs. We monitor the California legislature and will update this page if that changes.
