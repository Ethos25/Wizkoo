Philosophy

Learning is not a checklist
It's architecture

Thematic Threading

One idea becomes the lens for every subject. Volcanoes teach geography, chemistry, writing, and math. Not as separate boxes. As one connected investigation.

Spiral Learning

Week 8 references Week 3. Your child's knowledge compounds. Every new theme connects backward to what they've already explored.

Multi-Child Differentiation

Same room. Same theme. Different entry points. Your 7-year-old writes a report while your 2-year-old builds with playdough.

Capability Over Calendar

We don't sort children by grade. We meet them where they are. By name, by reading level, by what they're ready for next.
